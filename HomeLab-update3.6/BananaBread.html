Banana Bread

Tags

Ingredients

  • 8 T (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 c all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 c whole wheat flour
  • 1 t salt
  • t 1/2 t baking powder
  • 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 t vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. In a large bowl, mix together dry ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugar, then beat in eggs and bananas.
  4. Stir the banana mixture into the dry ingredients; do not mix more than necessary.
  5. Gently stir in vanilla.
  6. Pour mixture into greased loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 45-60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.

Variations

  1. Banana nut bread: Stir in 1/2 c chopped pecans or walnuts with vanilla.
  2. Banana muffins: Preheat oven to 400 F, pur batter into muffin tins, and bake for 20-30 minutes.

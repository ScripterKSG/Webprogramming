Simple Ribbed-Edge Beanie

Tags

Materials

  • 100 g worsted-weight yarn
  • US size 8 circular needles, 16"
  • US size 8 double-pointed needles

Instructions

  1. Cast on 80 stitches unsing the long-tailed cast on. Join in the round, being careful not to twist. Place marker at the beginning of the round.
  2. *Knit 2, purl 2, repeat from * around. Knit 10 rows of ribbing.
  3. Continue to knit in the round until the hat measures 8" from the cast-on edge.
  4. Row 1: * place marker, knit 8 stitches, k2tog, repeat from * 7 more times.
    Row 2: knit Repeat rows 1 and 2 until there are 8 stitches remaining, switching to double-pointed needles when needed.
  5. Break the yarn, thread the tail through the open stitches, and cinch. Weave in tail on the underside.
